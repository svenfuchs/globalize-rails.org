This project grew out of many discussions I had with Josh Sierles. A lot of the view translation stuff is inspired by Per Wigren’s MLR code. I also used a lot of his localization data to populate the globalize tables. I have no idea how he compiled all that data, but it’s great stuff.

A lot of people have helped iron out initial bugs (and I’m sure there will be many more opportunities to do the same), but I’d like to thank Andreas Roedl especially, for the many hours he’s put in.

Jeremy Voorhis and Olivier Amblet and Yann Lugrin (http://liquid-concept.ch) are the core dev faithful. Obviously, I wouldn’t be doing this if it weren’t for the phenom that is Ruby on Rails. Thanks, DHH.
